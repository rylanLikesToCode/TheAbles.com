Aaron Turner

Played by Jack Dylan Grazer

Description

Aaron Turner, played by Jack Dylan Grazer, is a 16 year old grove and Jonah's best friend. Aaron, along with Felicity, were some of the only people that supported Jonah while he was being ridiculed for lacking an able. Aaron plays an extremely important part in catching Blake and destroying AFUF. While being Wade's crush, he does not share the feelings. When catching Blake, Aaron is the first person to agree to keep Blake alive. He also gets trapped by Ghoul in their battle against AFUF, and Wade is able save his life, however sacrificing his own.
